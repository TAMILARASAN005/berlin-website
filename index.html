Our Campus

The new BSBI main campus is situated in the historical building of Alte Post. This building boasts a modern interior, with 17,000 square meters of space. The Neukölln district is a melting pot of cultures. With BSBI in the area, we will bring an even truer sense of diversity to this urban neighbourhood.

Apart from accessibility, the new campus offers modern equipment and facilities including an audio and visual studio, IT laboratory, cafeteria, lounge areas, rooftop access and terraces.
